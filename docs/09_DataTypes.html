Data types

Data can be stored in differet types: factors, characters, integer etc.

In tibbles we get the information directly printed

2 audi         a4      1.8  1999     4 manual(m5) f        21    29 p     compa…
3 audi         a4      2    2008     4 manual(m6) f        20    31 p     compa…

5 audi         a4      2.8  1999     6 auto(l5)   f        16    26 p     compa…
6 audi         a4      2.8  1999     6 manual(m5) f        18    26 p     compa…

We can also access it for each column

class(mpg$hwy)
[1] "integer"
